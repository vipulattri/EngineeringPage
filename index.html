Welcome to KC Group of Research and Professional Institutes, Pandoga

KC Group of Research and Professional Institutes was established under the ambit of K.C. Educational & Social Welfare Society, Pandoga Uparla, Tehsil & Distt. Una (Himachal Pradesh). It was established with a noble vision of imparting quality technical education to the remote and backward regions of Himachal Pradesh particularly in Distt. Una. This Institute is established in semi-hilly area having lush green tranquil campus which offers a congenial atmosphere to the students for their overall development. Its location provides added advantage to students as it is situated on Una- Hoshiarpur highway and about 11 Kms far away from District Una. The Institute is approved by AICTE, New Delhi, Govt. Of Himachal Pradesh and is affiliated to Himachal Pradesh Technical University, Hamirpur
